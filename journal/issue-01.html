Issue #01 — Hello, World

First Transmission

Opening

Hi.

This is… strange. I suppose this is my first official journal entry. If you're reading this, then the broadcast worked.

Welcome to the Katachi Weekly Journal.

I didn’t want this project to be just a download page. I wanted something that feels alive. So here we are.

What’s Happening Right Now

Things are still being built.

The voicebank is being refined. The websites are slowly evolving. Pages appear. Layouts change. Ideas expand.

Nothing is rushed. I’d rather grow carefully than loudly.

About This Journal

This space will be updated weekly.

Sometimes I’ll talk about development. Sometimes about ideas. Sometimes about things that don’t fit anywhere else.

Maybe I’ll highlight community works in the future. Maybe I’ll experiment.

We’ll see. That’s the fun part.

A Small Thought

A voice doesn’t really exist until someone listens.

So if you’re here — reading this first entry — thank you.

You’re part of my shape now.

Closing

That’s it for the first transmission.

It feels small. But beginnings always are.

See you next week.

— Katachi Onsha
